Game of Thrones is a medieval fantasy TV series created by D.B. Weiss and David Benioff. The show is based on A Song of Ice and Fire by George R.R. Martin. The series is currently on its 6th seasons, with each season being 10 episodes. The average runtime for an episode is about 60 minutes and airs on HBO. Game of Thrones is considered to be the most popular television show ever, with each season gaining millions of new viewers each year. The show has spawned many parodies and an active and always growing fanbase.
